Thịt Kho Recipe

Image Source: Hungry Huy

Description

Thịt kho is a comforting meal made of braised pork belly and eggs in a caramel sauce. I have memories of eating it over rice. It is sweet, salty, and savory. Perfect for winter meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb pork belly
  • water
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • silicone spatula
  • 8 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 yellow onion - chopped into eight

Instructions

Nước Màu (Caramel Sauce)

First, we need to make the caramel sauce that is used to color the thịt kho.

  1. To a saucepan on medium heat, add the sugar
  2. Stir the sugar continuously with the silicone spatula
  3. Continue to stir as the sugar melts and starts to turn dark
  4. Adjust the heat as needed and when the caramel begins to smoke, add the warm water slowly to the sugar and stir unti all of the sugar dissolves
  5. Set aside for later

Thịt Kho

Now we can begin cooking the thịt kho

  1. Cut the pork belly into 1 inch cubes
  2. Bring water in a pot to boil - enough to submerge the pork
  3. Add the pork for 2 minutes, then drain and rinse the pork
  4. Add the fish sauce, salt, and fresh water to cover the pork and bring to a boil
  5. Once the pot is boiling, reduce the heat and allow the pot to simmer for 1.5 hours
  6. For the first 40 minutes of simmering, keep the pot covered, stirring occasionally
  7. After 40 minutes, remove the cover to let the contents reduce
  8. After a total of 1.5 hours simmering, add the nước màu to the pot
  9. Add the hard-boiled eggs and onion
  10. Continue to reduce to about 1/3 the starting volume
  11. Check and season with salt or fish sauce or add water to taste
